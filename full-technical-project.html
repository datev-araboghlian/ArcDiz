Stage 2

3D Visualization

We create a detailed three-dimensional model of the space, allowing you to experience photorealistic visuals and gain a complete understanding of your future interior.

Professional 3D visualization is essential for establishing a clear and shared vision between the client and the designer. It also serves as a reliable reference for contractors and builders, supporting accurate execution, material selection, and attention to every technical detail.

At this stage, the designer carefully selects furniture, materials, and finishes from real, current manufacturer collections — ensuring that everything you see can be realistically implemented on site.

This phase allows full creative flexibility: colors can be refined, and decorative elements added or modified until the design feels exactly right within a normal limit.

Stage 3

Drawings

The composition of the documentation includes a basic set of drawings, it will be enough to control the process of some work at a construction site.

We adhere to high requirements in the preparation of working documentation.

The composition of the working documentation:
  • Existing Plan
  • Demolition Plan
  • New Wall Plan
  • Generic Furniture Plan
  • Electrical Drawings
  • Plumbing Plan
  • HVAC
  • Interior Elevation Plan
  • Interior Elevations (Essential Ones)
  • Door Schedules
  • Floor Finishing Plan
  • Wall Finishing Plan
  • Skirting Plan
  • Section(s)
  • Reflected Ceiling Plan
