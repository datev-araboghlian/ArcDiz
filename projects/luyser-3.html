Luyser Residential Complex

2-room apartment 50 m²
Yerevan

Minimalism finds its warmest expression in this thoughtfully composed interior, where clean lines and a restrained palette of creamy whites, soft beiges, and natural wood tones create a space that feels both refined and deeply livable. Every element serves a purpose — flat-front cabinetry, flush built-ins, and hidden storage eliminate visual noise while smoked black glass accents add quiet depth and contrast.

Warm LED lighting tucked beneath open shelves casts a gentle glow, softening the precision of the architecture. The fluted round dining table and bouclé-upholstered chairs introduce subtle texture, reminding us that minimalism here is not cold or stark, but intentional and human. This is a space designed to breathe — where less is not a compromise, but a carefully considered luxury.
