Greybadov Residential Complex

2-room apartment 79 m²
Yerevan

Contemporary Scandinavian design defines this interior through its clean architectural lines, soft neutral palette, and balanced combination of warmth and minimalism. The space embraces Scandinavian simplicity while introducing modern detailing and refined finishes that create a sophisticated yet comfortable atmosphere.

Light wood flooring, warm beige tones, and soft textured materials establish a calm and inviting environment, while black accents and sleek lighting fixtures add contrast and a contemporary character. The furniture selection emphasizes functionality and comfort, maintaining a clean visual flow throughout the open layout.
