Arshakunyats 192

4-room apartment 120 m²
Yerevan

Contemporary minimalism shaped the vision of this interior through clean architectural lines, warm natural materials, and a refined balance between functionality and aesthetics. The combination of walnut wood textures, soft neutral tones, black accents, and integrated lighting creates a modern atmosphere that feels elegant, calm, and welcoming at the same time.

Every element was carefully selected to maintain visual simplicity while adding depth and character to the space. From the open kitchen composition to the dining area and custom shelving details, the design focuses on creating a timeless living experience with a strong contemporary identity and a warm residential feel.
