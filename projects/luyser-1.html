Luyser Residential Complex

2-room apartment 49 m²
Yerevan

Scandinavian design defines this interior through clean architectural lines, warm wood finishes, and a soft neutral palette that creates a calm and inviting atmosphere. The design emphasizes simplicity, functionality, and comfort, where every element serves a purpose while maintaining a refined visual balance.

Integrated storage solutions and built-in cabinetry enhance the minimalist character of the space, keeping the interior visually clean and uncluttered. Natural materials, soft textures, and carefully selected decorative elements add warmth and depth without overpowering the overall composition.

Light tones combined with warm wooden finishes create a bright and timeless aesthetic, while the balanced use of textures introduces a cozy and welcoming feeling. The result is a sophisticated Scandinavian interior that feels modern, functional, and effortlessly elegant.
