Luyser Residential Complex

4-room apartment 112 m²
Yerevan

Minimalism defines this interior through pure geometric forms, a restrained material palette, and a strong focus on spatial clarity. Soft neutral tones, black architectural accents, and integrated lighting elements work together to create a calm and sophisticated atmosphere with a refined contemporary character.

The design emphasizes simplicity without compromising comfort, combining textured furnishings, clean-lined cabinetry, and subtle lighting details to achieve a balanced and timeless composition. Large openings, minimal decorative elements, and carefully selected materials allow the space to feel open, elegant, and intentionally curated from every angle.
