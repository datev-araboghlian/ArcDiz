Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the basic package?
Our basic package includes 2D floor plans, basic 3D visualization, and 2 revision rounds. Perfect for those looking to get a solid foundation for their project.
How long does each package take?
Delivery times vary by package: Basic (14 days), Professional (21 days), and Premium (30 days). These timeframes ensure quality work while meeting your deadlines.
Can I upgrade my package later?
Yes, you can upgrade to a higher package at any time. We'll simply charge the difference and adjust the deliverables and timeline accordingly.

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Payment plans are available for Professional and Premium packages.
