Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a project?
Project timelines vary based on complexity. Basic designs typically take 2-3 weeks, while comprehensive projects may take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you offer revisions in your packages?
Yes, all our packages include revision rounds. Basic includes 1 revision, Professional includes 2 revisions, and Premium includes unlimited revisions within the project scope.
Can I upgrade my package later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher tier package at any time during the project. We'll simply adjust the pricing to reflect the upgrade.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and digital payments. For larger projects, we can arrange a flexible payment schedule.
Do you work on international projects?
Yes, we work with clients worldwide. Our digital workflow and communication tools allow us to collaborate effectively across different time zones.
