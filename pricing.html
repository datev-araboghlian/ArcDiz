FAQ

Why is supervision necessary?
Project implementation control is necessary so that you get the most accurate and successful result. Only architectural supervision will ensure 100% implementation of the project into reality.
Do you take into account the financial capabilities of the customer?
Of course, this is the main condition for successful cooperation. We always ask you to clearly indicate the amount beyond which the customer is not ready to go.
Is it possible to start repairs in parallel with the development of a design project?
Yes, but we recommend completing the design project first to avoid costly changes during construction.
What styles do you work in?
We work in all styles - from classic to modern minimalism. Our team adapts to your preferences and lifestyle.
Can you create a complete project in 1 month?
The timeline depends on the project scope. Small projects can be completed in 3-4 weeks, larger ones require 2-3 months.
Do you provide services remotely? And if so, how is architectural supervision ensured in this case?
Yes, services can be provided remotely. We use video calls, detailed documentation, and trusted local partners for supervision.
Why do you need a set of objects?
A set of objects allows us to create a cohesive design throughout your space, ensuring visual harmony.
Do you have proven construction crews you can trust? And do you work with the customer's team?
Both options are possible. We have our own experienced team, but also work with the customer's team when preferred.
If you have not received the keys to the apartment yet, can you start working on the project?
Yes, we can start with floor plans and preliminary designs while you wait for access to the property.
How many floor plans do you make?
We typically provide 2-3 layout options for you to choose from, which we then refine based on your feedback.
Are engineering projects included in the working documentation?
The documentation does not include specialized engineering projects, but they can be performed by our contractors upon request.
Only one room design is needed, do you provide such a service?
Absolutely! We offer single-room design services with the same attention to detail as full-home projects.
