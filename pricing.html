FAQ

Why is supervision necessary?
Project implementation control is necessary so that you get the most accurate and successful result. Otherwise, there's a lot of difficulties, the potential for chaos/mismatch: materials, colors of objects/elements.
Do you take into account the financial capabilities of the customer?
Of course this is the main condition for successful consultation. We always ask you to clarify indicate the amount beyond which the customer is not ready to go.
Do you have construction crews you can trust?
Both options are possible, the main thing for the ideal implementation of the project is the constructed actions of the customer.
